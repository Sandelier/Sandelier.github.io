About me

I am a 20-year-old developer and aspiring 3D artist from Finland with vocational qualification in information and communications technology.

Coding

At present, my primary coding language is Javascript/Node.js.

I do know some Python and Java also.

I license all my projects with MIT License since i like to share my code and allow other people to modify and benefit from it.

Back story

My interest in coding started during junior high school while creating Minecraft scripts using the Script plugin in 2017.

After a hiatus of about year or two, I delved into actual coding.

I began with Java and i used it for around two months before transitioning briefly to Python. Finally i found Javascript/Node.js which became my primary language.

3D

Currently my main 3D software is Blender. I do have some familiarity with Fusion 360 as well.

Similar to coding, i believe in sharing, so all my 3D creations are shared with CC BY 4.0.

Backstory

My journey to 3D began in July 2023 when i attended a two-week coure that introduced me to Blender.

Back then it was just curiosity. However, in August 2023 during a five-month 3D course, my curiosity changed into passion for Blender.

During the same course, i also learned to use Fusion 360

I enjoy creating mostly cute or horror-themed stuff in Blender, but my knowledge expands beyond just those genres.
